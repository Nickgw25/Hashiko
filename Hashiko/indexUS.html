Company History

The company came up with the perception that in Brazil, there are more than 30 million people aged 60 and over, this large portion of the population had to adapt to remote activities and the distance during the isolation period. And it was from these data presented by IBGE, that the main idea of ​​the company emerged.

Company Mission

“Bring comfort, safety and facilitate the inclusion of elderly people in the area of ​​technology.”

How do we accomplish this?

With a company focused on applications with adapted languages, different font sizes and buttons, colors, video explanatory, animations, clear and simple information.

Company Vision

“To be an application that serves as a model in the care and care of the elderly and family, developing 100% of its activities with technique, humanity, understanding and sustainability, taking into account the individual needs of each one. Our goal is to create a world where everyone can access technology.”

Application Proposal

Especially aimed at the Elderly, with it, it will be possible to teach people aged 60 or over how to use the basic functions of a smartphone through a mascot, the Hachiko. It will assist the elderly in tasks such as: reading texts, organizing schedules, medication reminders and physical exercises, besides offering company with conversations, stories, and jokes.
